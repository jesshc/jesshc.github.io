The Radiological Accident in Goiânia

The Accident Spread & Decontamination Outlook

Outlook

The Goiania incident teaches us an important lesson about the effects of radioactive contamination. The medical histories of the victims provide new insight into the effects of radioactivity on human bodies. Unfortunately, the long-term follow-ups of the victims were never published so we’ll never know the true extent of the health impact. Today, untraceable amounts of Cesium-137 continue to seep into Goiania’s groundwater supplies through the contaminated soil underneath the concrete. The demolition of buildings and other waste from the decontamination would have to be stored in lead containers for more than 180 years. Hopefully we can learn from this accident and prevent this type of incident in the future.
